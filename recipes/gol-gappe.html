Golgappe

Desciption

Golgappe is a north indian dish that is widely popular and especially enjoyed during the summer. It is a small ball made from flour that is hollow from the inside.

It is normally served by stuffing the inside full of a mashed potatoes and chickpeas stuffing and then dipped in minty and tangy water.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Water
  • Vegetable Oil
  • Potatoes
  • Chickpeas
  • Red Onions
  • Chilli Powder
  • Salt
  • Mint
  • Corrainder Powder

Steps

  1. First we make mix water and flour to make dough.
  2. Dough is then cut into small balls and fried in oil.
  3. Potatoes are boiled and peeled.
  4. Chickpeas are also boiled in water until they soften.
  5. We mix mashed potatoes, crused chickpeas and chopped red onions.
  6. You can add salt and other spicies to this filling according to your taste.
  7. Mix various spicies and mint in a couple of litres of water to suit your taste.
